Why Did You Choose This Project

I chose this project because income inequality is something that affects millions of people yet is often misunderstood. I wanted to explore what factors — like education, age, and occupation — actually influence whether someone earns above or below a certain threshold. Data felt like the best way to cut through assumptions and look at the patterns objectively. This topic also felt personally relevant, as conversations about economic opportunity come up a lot in my community, and I wanted to back those conversations with real numbers.

Why Did You Choose This Project

I chose this project because mental health is something that affects students every day, yet it is rarely talked about openly. I wanted to use real data to understand what factors — like GPA and year of study — are connected to depression among students. Data felt like the most objective way to explore this topic and turn it into something meaningful. I created this project as part of the Data4Change course.

Student Mental Health Dataset

The dataset used in this project is the Student Mental Health dataset from Kaggle. It contains survey responses from university students covering GPA, year of study, gender, and self-reported mental health conditions including depression, anxiety, and panic attacks. It can be accessed at:

https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/shariful07/student-mental-health →

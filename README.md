# Student Mental Health Analysis
### Data4Change Final Project — Vivaan Seela

## About
An analysis of university student survey data exploring how GPA, 
year of study, and anxiety relate to depression among students.

## Research Questions
1. Do students with depression tend to have a lower GPA than students without depression?
2. Which year of study has the highest rate of depression among university students?
3. Are students with anxiety more likely to also have depression?
